"The Girl with a Pearl Earring" is a renowned painting by the Dutch artist Johannes Vermeer. It is a portrait of a young woman with a pearl earring, characterized by its captivating use of light and shadow. The painting is held in the Mauritshuis museum in The Hague, Netherlands, and is considered a masterpiece of Dutch Golden Age painting.

The Kiss (in German Der Kuss) is an oil-on-canvas painting with added gold leaf, silver and platinum by the Austrian Symbolist painter Gustav Klimt.
